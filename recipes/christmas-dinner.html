Christmas Dinner

The perfect meal for Christmas Day

This traditional festive roast will please all your guests, and includes enough trimmings to feed a hungry crowd!

Ingredients

  • 1 turkey
  • 1 gammon
  • Roast potatoes
  • Pigs in blankets
  • Stuffing
  • Vegetables
  • Gravy

Steps

  1. Cook the meal in the oven
  2. Roast the potatoes
  3. Fry the pigs in blankets
  4. Boil the vegetables
  5. Heat up the gravy
