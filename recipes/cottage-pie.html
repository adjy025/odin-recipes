Cottage Pie

A hearty, meaty potato pie

When the winter months roll around, there's nothing better than a cottage pie. A rich beef filling topped with cheesy mash, this simple dish can be brought together in an hour, including cooking time.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince (15% fat)
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Bay leaf
  • Mixed herbs
  • Tomato puree
  • Beef stock
  • 300g mashed potato
  • 150g grated cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Fry the beef, carrot and onion together until mince is browned
  2. Add the garlic, tomato puree and mixed herbs and sautee for 2 minutes
  3. Add the beef stock and bring the pot to a simmer
  4. Allow the mixture to reduce, stirring occasionally
  5. When the mince mixture has thickened, add to a baking dish and top with the mashed potato and grated cheese
  6. Bake at 180c for 35 minutes, or until the cheese has turned golden brown
  7. Allow to rest for 10 minutes before cutting and serving
