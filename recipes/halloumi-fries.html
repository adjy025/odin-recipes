Halloumi Fries

A cheesy alternative to chips, perfect for dipping

This versatile Cypriot hard cheese is perfect for coating in batter and frying and goes great with a variety of sources, including tzatziki!

Ingredients

  • 250g block of halloumi
  • Flour
  • Paprika
  • Carbonated water
  • Oil, for frying

Steps

  1. Slice the halloumi into thumb-sized fries and set aside on kitchen paper to dry
  2. Mix the flour and paprika in a mixing bowl with the carbonated water
  3. Dip the fries into the batter mixture and let the excess drip off
  4. Add to a hot pot of oil or a deep-fat fryer until fries have turned golden brown
  5. Season with salt and serve with a dipping sauce like tzatziki
